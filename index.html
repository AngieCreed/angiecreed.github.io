About Me

I have spent the majority of my career thus far as a high school counselor and Director of Guidance. I have a natural proclivity and curiosity for things of a technical nature and strengths in data analysis.

Over the last three years I have been seeking a new career path outside of secondary education. After some research, boldly, I am taking my 15+ years of professional work experience and directing my career focus into web and software development.

I graduated from the JavaScript cohort program at Eleven Fifty Academy in November, 2018. I enjoyed all that I learned there, and have since been working to solidify and build upon the skills that I acquired.

I am seeking a position as a front end developer, but would ultimately like to become full-stack. I am open to learning new technologies in a company or business organization that will work to help in my development toward that end.
